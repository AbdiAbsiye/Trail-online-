Hamilton Lake

Join a peaceful virtual walk around Hamilton Lake also known as (Rotoroa), a unique nature spot in Waikato, New Zealand. It was Formed long time ago, it is important to Māori community, and now it’s a relaxing public park. Our video guides you along the 3.8km flat path, by showing you where people walk, cycle, and enjoy water activities like: -kayaking, paddle boating, and dragon boating, all with lovely green surroundings.

The reason I choose Hamilton Lake for this video is that it’s one of the most viewing placing in Hamilton Waikato. Its calm, pretty views and gentle activity make it a great place to unwind, especially for those unable to visit. This video aims to share the calming, beautiful feeling of this Waikato spot, offering you a peaceful break.

Lake Ngaroto

Discover Lake Ngaroto, a serene freshwater treasure in New Zealand's Waikato region! This historic lake, rich in Māori heritage, offers peaceful waters surrounded by lush native bush. Our virtual experience brings you the tranquil beauty of this special place - watch gentle ripples dance across the surface, hear native birdsong, and soak in the calm atmosphere from anywhere.

A haven for nature lovers, Lake Ngaroto invites you to explore its walking trails, spot rare wildlife, or simply reflect by its quiet shores. Let this virtual journey transport you to one of Waikato's most peaceful natural retreats.

Mount Maunganui

Mauao by Māori, offers breathtaking ocean views and golden beaches. Our virtual tour brings you the mountain's peaceful energy - hike summit trails, feel the sea breeze, and watch spectacular sunsets from anywhere.

People visit Mount Maunganui to surf its sparkling waves, relax in geothermal hot pools, or stroll along pristine shores. The mountain's energy is infectious, inviting exploration and relaxation in equal measure. Through this virtual experience, you can wander its winding paths, watch the sunset over the Pacific, and connect with nature's beauty—wherever you are. Mount Maunganui is more than just a landmark; it's a place of rejuvenation and wonder, now brought to life for you to enjoy anytime, anywhere.
